How to move

By Bike

For a complete dutch experience, nothing better than riding a bike for a few hours on Amsterdam's canals. You can either rent a bike or take a guided cycle tour. Cycling is a fantastic way to discover the city, scape for the traffic and do a good work out.

By Tram

The easiest way to travel around Amsterdam is by tram or metro. Tram is one of the most popular public transport and can take you to almost every part of the city. Many of the city's trams terminate at Amsterdam Central Station, and you can check the itinerary via their app.

By Boat

Amsterdam's charm is in its canals, the fantastic and historical buildings together with intelligent structures of the dikes which makes it almost impossible to go to Amsterdam and not take a boat trip.
